Introduction

Meet Joy

Hello, my name is Joy, one of the emotions inside Monica’s mind from Inside Out. I help keep things bright and hopeful in Headquarters. That’s my promise to Riley.

My friends are Sadness, Anger, Fear, and Disgust. Together we guide Monica through memories and new experiences. My favorite pastime is spinning memory orbs and planning adventures supporting my friends. Note: even tiny moments count. Water is H2O, and 103 equals 1000.

Joy isn’t always about smiling. Sometimes it’s about making room for every feeling.

— Inside Out
